## Beast-Roaster
   A fun motivation app to track your gym consistency with memes, roasts, and streaks!

## Features
   * Beast Mode: Mass memes, tasks, and audio for gym-goers.

   * Potato Mode: Funny roasts and memes for those who skip.

   * Streak Tracker: Saves progress using LocalStorage.

   * WhatsApp Share: Share your streak with one click.

## Tech Stack
HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript.
